## FULL DESCRIPTION (Google Play / App Store, 4000 chars max)

ClearPass is the smarter way to prepare for your UK DVSA driving theory test. Whether you're just starting out or have failed before, ClearPass adapts to how you learn and helps you pass first time.

**700+ Official-Style Questions**
Practice with a comprehensive bank of DVSA-style questions across all 14 theory test topics - from Alertness and Hazard Awareness to Motorway Rules and Road Signs. The adaptive algorithm identifies your weakest areas and gives you more of what you need most.

**AI Tutor Explains Every Answer**
Don't just mark answers right or wrong - understand why. Our AI tutor powered by Claude gives clear, concise explanations tailored to UK driving rules and the Highway Code. Ask follow-up questions in plain English.

**Full Mock Tests**
Take timed 50-question mock tests that mirror the real DVSA format exactly. Detailed results show your performance by topic, your pass probability, and where to focus next.

**Hazard Perception Practice**
Train your eye for danger with hazard perception clips designed to match the real test format. Essential for passing the full theory test.

**88 Official UK Road Signs**
Browse and learn all official UK road signs using real DVSA images. Filter by category and test yourself with the road signs practice mode.

**Track Every Step of Your Journey**
- Daily study streaks keep you consistent
- XP points and level-up rewards keep you motivated
- Topic mastery badges unlock as you improve
- Pass probability score updates with every session
- 14-topic performance breakdown shows exactly where you stand
- Speed Round mode: 10 questions, 90 seconds - earn bonus XP

**Weak Spot Drilling**
Our weak spot mode identifies questions you've answered wrong twice and drills you until you've answered each one correctly three times in a row. It's the fastest way to eliminate gaps.

**Accessible by Design**
ClearPass was built with accessibility at its core:
- OpenDyslexic font option for dyslexic learners
- Text-to-Speech reads questions aloud
- High contrast mode
- Large text option
- Cream background option to reduce glare

**Instructor & Parent Dashboard**
Driving instructors can monitor their pupils' progress, add lesson notes, and track readiness. Parents can receive weekly email progress updates.

**Study Smart, Not Hard**
Spaced repetition scheduling ensures you review material at the optimal time. Set your test date and ClearPass builds a personalised study plan day by day.

Join thousands of learners who have used ClearPass to prepare for their DVSA driving theory test. Whether your test is next week or next month, ClearPass gives you everything you need to walk in confident.
