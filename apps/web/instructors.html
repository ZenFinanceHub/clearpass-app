Common questions

Instructor FAQ

How do I get set up?

Sign up for a free account, then request instructor access from your profile. We’ll get you set up with your dashboard and referral link.

Do my pupils need to be Pro for me to see their progress?

No — you can see basic progress for any pupil connected to your dashboard, whether they’re on the free plan or Pro.

What happens after my first 3 months?

Your Pro access stays free as long as you’re actively helping pupils — either 2 new Pro conversions a month, or 10 active Pro pupils overall. We’ll let you know well in advance if that’s at risk.

How and when do I get paid?

Referral earnings are calculated monthly based on your pupils’ active Pro subscriptions and paid out directly.
